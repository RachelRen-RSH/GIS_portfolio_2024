Transportation

Efficient transportation systems are blood vessels in cities, playing a key role in promoting sustainable urban growth and improving the overall quality of life for residents. GIS analysis can efficiently ensure commuter's safety and enhance connectivity between cities.

Nature Conservancy

Fast urban development generates conflicts with wildlife coexistence. Remote sensing provides accurate data in analyzing timely changes on landuse, helping us make strategies in reducing natural hazards and protect wildlife habitat.

View Details

Public Health/Safety

Public health and safety not only directly impact the quality of life of individuals, but reveal the disparities of public amenties between communities as well. By analyzing spatial patterns, GIS can identify areas with high incidences of health problems, show the correlations between environmental factors and health outcomes, and highlight regions with limited access to public facilities.

View details

Housing

Increasing affordable housing contributes to economic stability and reduces poverty, creating an equal environment for employment and diversity. GIS and machine learning track the housing prices and make predictions about the price trend. It enables policymakers to identify underserved areas, target housing interventions, and allocate resources more effectively.
